REPLYMATE ★

AI Review Manager — Setup Guide

What You Need

Two free accounts:
1. Netlify — where your app lives (free plan is fine)
2. Anthropic Console — where your AI key comes from

REPLYMATE runs entirely in your browser — nothing is stored on a server. Your business profile saves locally on your device. Review content you paste is sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses, then discarded.

Step 1 — Deploy to Netlify (2 minutes)

1
Go to app.netlify.com/drop

Sign up for a free account if you don't have one. Takes 30 seconds.

2
Drag replymate-v5-netlify.zip onto the drop zone

Netlify unzips and deploys automatically. Done in about 20 seconds.

3
You'll get a URL — rename it to something you'll remember

Site Settings → Domain Management → Edit site name. Example: marco-replymate.netlify.app

4
Bookmark the URL on your phone and desktop

Next time you get a review, you're one tap away from a professional response.

Step 2 — Get Your Anthropic API Key (3 minutes)

1
Go to console.anthropic.com/settings/keys

Create a free Anthropic account if you don't have one.

2
Click "Create Key" and copy it right away

The key starts with sk-ant-api03-.... Copy it — you won't see it again after closing the page.

3
Add a small amount of API credit under Billing

$5 will last a very long time. REPLYMATE costs roughly $0.002 per review response pair (about $0.05 per week for an active business).

Step 3 — Set Up REPLYMATE

1
Open your REPLYMATE URL

You'll see a yellow banner asking for your API key.

2
Click "Add Key →", paste your key, hit Save

Stored in your browser only — never transmitted to our servers.

3
Fill in Your Business profile — takes 2 minutes, saves forever

Business name, type, tone, sign-off, and escalation contact. You only do this once.

4
Hit "Try an example →" to see it work, or paste your first review

Using REPLYMATE Daily

TabWhat it does
★ ReplyPaste a review → get 2 professional response options in seconds

For each review: Select the platform, click the star rating, optionally add the reviewer's name, paste the review text. The AI will auto-analyze the review (you'll see complaints, praise, and urgency chips appear). Hit Generate. Pick an option, edit if needed, copy, paste into Google/Yelp/etc.

💡 Smart Snippets: Add your recurring topics — parking, wait times, a specific policy — and REPLYMATE will weave in the right response automatically whenever that topic comes up. Set it once, saves forever.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my review content private?
Review text is sent from your browser to Anthropic's API to generate responses. It's not stored on any server we control. See Anthropic's privacy policy.

Can I use REPLYMATE on my phone?
Yes — the layout is mobile-responsive. Bookmark the URL on your phone so you can respond to reviews the moment they come in.

Does it work for multiple locations?
Your current license covers one business profile. To use for multiple locations, deploy separate instances with different business profiles.

What if the responses don't sound right?
Fill in more detail in Voice Note and add a Sign-Off. Hit Regenerate as many times as you like — each generation is a fresh attempt.
